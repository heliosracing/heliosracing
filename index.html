HELIOS RACING

Innovating for the F1 in Schools Competition

Overview

Helios Racing is an ambitious and innovative racing team competing in the prestigious Stem Racing competition. Our team of dedicated secondary school students from Cheltenham Ladies College is passionate about engineering, design, and STEM. We are committed to pushing the boundaries of performance and creativity to achieve excellence both on and off the track.

Helios Racing was chosen as it represents a modern, sci-fi sound that fits an engineering team. It is unique, easy to remember, and important for branding.

Meaning and Story Behind the Name

Helios, the Greek god of the Sun, symbolizes power, speed, and unwavering consistency. Named after the deity who drove a blazing chariot across the sky each day, Helios Racing represents our commitment to energy-driven innovation, precision engineering, and leading the way forward.

Our Mission

To design, manufacture, and race a miniature Formula 1 car that combines cutting-edge engineering principles, aerodynamic efficiency, and striking visual design. Through teamwork and collaboration, we aim to deliver a car that excels on the track while embodying the spirit of F1 innovation.

Our Strategy

  • Innovation in Design: Using advanced CAD software and wind tunnel simulations to refine every curve and component of our car.
  • Precision Manufacturing: Leveraging CNC machining and 3D printing for a high-quality, lightweight build.
  • Sustainable Practices: Incorporating eco-friendly materials and minimizing waste in our processes.
  • Community Engagement: Building partnerships with sponsors, promoting STEM education, and inspiring the next generation of innovators.

Communication

Having good communication within a team is a vital part of a competition like this. We prioritised having good communication links with each other as well as our sponsors, allowing us to work through any issue we may face collectively.

Team Page: By creating a WhatsApp group chat, we are able to share thoughts and ideas, as well as updates on our individual progress with the rest of the team. It also provides a collective space to store important documents for everyone to access.

Meetings: Our group meets once a week to discuss our progress and work through any issues that may have arisen during the week. Meeting face-to-face allows better communication, as well as helping others with different aspects of the competition.

Urgent Messages: If an urgent message needs to be sent during the week, we may call each other to make decisions or work through a problem.

Sponsorships

By partnering with companies, we can expand our opportunities and accomplish even more as a team. For example, we spend our sponsorship money on uniform clothing for our competitions. This makes us look put together and professional, helping us stand out from other teams. The money also goes toward transport costs, entry fees, material costs, and other vital expenses that enable us to compete at our best. Our relationship with our sponsors is mutually beneficial; in return for sponsoring us, companies receive promotion via our Instagram, as well as their logo on our car and all team clothing.
